# Campaign Brief: QuienTiene.com

**Generated:** 2026-05-12  
**Website:** https://quientiene.com  
**Platforms:** Meta, Google, TikTok, LinkedIn, Microsoft  
**Objective:** Awareness + Lead Generation (funnel approach: cold → conversion)  
**Concepts:** 4  
**Copy Framework:** PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solve) for awareness; 4P (Promise-Picture-Proof-Push) for conversion

---

## Brand DNA Summary

QuienTiene es una plataforma tech-forward que conecta propietarios de autos con repuestos verificados en una red de tiendas confiables. La marca transmite seguridad, velocidad e innovación a través de un diseño futurista (rojo + negro + tipografía Orbitron) y un tono directo, boldente y accesible. Target: propietarios de autos 25-55 años y mecánicos que buscan resolver problemas rápidamente sin fraude.

---

## Audit Context

**Key Findings from Landing Page Audit:**
1. **Message Match Gap**: Sin galería de repuestos ni logos de tiendas, los visitantes no entienden "qué" se vende
2. **Trust Signals Missing**: Falta proof visual de la red de tiendas y número de repuestos disponibles  
3. **Product Showcase**: CRÍTICO - zero imágenes de repuestos o tiendas. Esto mata 15-25% de conversión potencial

**Concepts Designed to Address:**
- Concept 1 (Problem) & 2 (Speed) → Agitan el dolor
- Concept 3 (Network) → Prueban la red y confianza
- Concept 4 (Solution) → Cierran con CTA clara

## Campaign Concepts

### Concept 1: "El Problema Sin Fin"
**Hypothesis:** Cold audience responds to problem recognition. Once they see their pain reflected, they'll listen to the solution.  
**Primary Message:** "Buscar repuestos es un dolor: falsificaciones, tiendas cerradas, precios escondidos. QuienTiene resuelve eso en 5 minutos."  
**Tone:** Sympathetic, knowledgeable, confident  
**Visual Direction:** Split-screen or carousel showing the pain (confused driver + phone calls + fake parts) on one side, then the calm solution (app + quick answers) on the other. Dark, relatable imagery. Use red accent to highlight the contrast.  
**Target Platforms:** Meta (carousel), Google (search intent), TikTok (relatable storytelling)  
**CTA:** "Encuentra tu repuesto sin complicaciones"  
**Addresses:** Message match gap—makes the problem concrete so solution feels necessary  
**Copy Framework:** PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solve)

---

### Concept 2: "5 Minutos"
**Hypothesis:** Speed is a unique insight. Most competitors don't promise response time. Lead with velocity.  
**Primary Message:** "Cotizaciones de repuestos en 5 minutos. Tiendas verificadas. Precios transparentes. Cero fraude."  
**Tone:** Direct, modern, energetic  
**Visual Direction:** Dynamic motion: clock ticking → WhatsApp message → instant response from a store with logo visible. Use neon red accent (#FF1744) with geometric shapes and glitch effects (matches brand). Fast-paced, mobile-optimized.  
**Target Platforms:** TikTok (scroll-stopping speed), Meta (carousel/video), Google (search)  
**CTA:** "Envía tu solicitud ahora"  
**Addresses:** Product showcase—show repuestos and stores responding in real-time  
**Copy Framework:** 4P (Promise-Picture-Proof-Push)

---

### Concept 3: "Red Verificada + Logos"
**Hypothesis:** Trust is built by seeing real partners. Logo-driven concept proves market fit and reduces fraud fear.  
**Primary Message:** "Conectado a 45+ tiendas verificadas, 500+ repuestos disponibles. Compra con seguridad."  
**Tone:** Professional, reassuring, confident  
**Visual Direction:** Grid/carousel of partner store logos (Bosch, Michelin, major local brands) with part counts and ratings. Overlay with badges: "Verified", "Anti-fraud", "24/7 Support". Use clean white text on dark (brand-compliant). Include one hero image of a real mechanic or driver looking satisfied + their quote.  
**Target Platforms:** Meta (grid/carousel dominant), LinkedIn (B2B sellers), Google (search)  
**CTA:** "Ver tiendas disponibles en tu zona"  
**Addresses:** Trust signals + product showcase (this is THE concept that fixes landing page)  
**Copy Framework:** 4P (Proof-heavy variant)

---

### Concept 4: "Vendedores: Multiplica ventas"
**Hypothesis:** Dual-audience split. Sellers respond to ROI metrics. Drive seller registrations to grow network, which grows buyer trust.  
**Primary Message:** "Tiendas de repuestos: aumenta ventas hasta 20%. Recupera tu inversión en la primera venta. Red nacional. Barato: $10/mes."  
**Tone:** Entrepreneurial, direct, results-focused  
**Visual Direction:** B2B style: tablet/dashboard showing incoming requests, matched replies, and sales metrics (upward curve). Feature a store owner with happy expression closing a deal. Use professional B2B color palette (dark + red accent) with charts/stats overlay.  
**Target Platforms:** LinkedIn (sellers), Meta (local business targeting), Google (high-intent search: "vender repuestos online")  
**CTA:** "Registra tu tienda gratis"  
**Addresses:** Growing the supply side = more buyers trust the network  
**Copy Framework:** 4P (Promise-heavy for seller ROI)
